User Experience Designer, Deloitte Digital; Seattle, WA - Jan 2014 - July 2014

I designed the user experience for an iPad app called “Tetra” , which allowed the user to create a “flow” of Tableau Software, and QlikView visualizations, and then add his or her own story or commentary as an overlay, in order to share and communicate a trend or story with another end user. I facilitated weekly wireframe reviews with the client, balancing their expectations, and incorporating feedback.
I also co-designed the user experience for an internal iOS iPhone app called “Concierge”, which, through the use of geofencing, Bluetooth LE, and wall-mounted iPads within the office, guided our clients from the airport, directly to the relevant conference room. It dynamically provided useful information such as who they were meeting, how to navigate the office to the conference room, restaurants in Fremont that studio members recommend to eat at, and even listed the ever changing restroom access codes.
